Cost added each time the bow moves to a different string. Higher → solver hugs one string; lower → freely hops strings for shorter finger stretches.

Position shift 3

Cost per semitone of hand travel up or down the neck. Higher → fewer large leaps; lower → allows distant positions for cleaner string choices.

High position 0.3

Cost proportional to how far up the neck the hand sits. Higher → favours 1st–3rd position (easier for most players); lower → freely uses upper positions.

Pinky penalty 1
higher → avoids pinky

Extra cost whenever the pinky (4th finger) is assigned. Higher → solver avoids demanding stretches; lower → pinky shares load evenly with other fingers.

Open string -2
higher → avoids open strings

Negative → favours open strings (resonant tone, bow hand free to shift). Positive → avoids open strings (useful for continuous legato or vibrato throughout).

Unconventional pos 4

Penalty for half-positions (1½, 2½, …). Higher → sticks to standard 1st, 3rd, 5th, 7th positions; lower → allows any position for finer pitch control.
